Settings

The settings view allow you to control your informations. You can change your name, your password or delete your account. To go back to the previews view, click on the back icon in the menu.

How do I change my name ?

To change your name, press on the edit icon on the right of your current name. You will then be prompted to type in a new name. Press on the tick icon to validate the change. Press on the cross icon to cancel the change.

How do I change my password ?

To change your password, press on the big green button that reads "Change my password". You will then be prompted to type in your current password for security reasons and your new password two times to prevent typing errors. Press on the tick icon to validate the change or on the cross icon to cancel it.
Do not lose the new password, you will need it to connect to the application !

How do I delete my account ?

To delete your account, press on the big green button that reads "delete my account". You will be asked to type in your password and confirm your choice. Be aware that this action is irreversible. All your booking will be lost.
