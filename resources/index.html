HELP

Authentification

How do I log in ?

To log in, you need to type in your name and your password in the adjacent fields and then, press the green button reading "Log in".

I do not have an account

If you do not have an account, press on the "register" link at the bottom of the screen to be redirected to the register view.

I lost my password

If you have lost your password, please contact your local administrator to reset it.

Registration

How do I register ?

To register, you need to type in your name and your password. To prevent typing errors, you need to enter the password twice. Then click on the green button reading "register".

I already have an account

If you already have an account, press on the "log in" link at the bottom og the screen to be redirected to the login view.

Search

This view allows you to select the criteria that interests you in the vehicle your are searching.

The view is composed of 3 major components. The breadcrumb, the criterion selector and the button bar at the bottom

I want to select a criterion

The criteria are organized in "groups" called categories. In the head of the breadcrumb is displayed the name of the category you are curretly in. To switch to another one, press on the name and pick the one that interests you in the list. To select a criterion, press on its name on the right part on the breadcrumb. You can also use the left and right icons at the bottom of the screen to navigate between the criteria.
Be aware that once you have seen a criterion, it becomes used for your research. if you want to prevent this, press on the "this criterion is not useful to me" button before changing the current criterion.

This criterion is really important to me

By checking this option, you specify that the vehicle that you want must totally match this criterion.

How do I use the range selector ?

To use the range selector, drag the lower knog to the min value that you want to match and the upper knob to the max value that you want. be aware that if you cannot set both knob to the max value of the range or it will get stuck. Our developers are kinda lazy. The selected range is highlighted.

How do I use the option selector ?

The option selector shows a list of the options proposed by this criterion. You can select one or more options.
To select an option, press one time on it. it will become highlighted. To remove the option, press on it again, it will fall back to its default state.

How do I use the tree selector ?

The tree selector show a list of the root values. To select a node, click on it. To unselect it along with all its children, press on it again. Note that if you select a node but none of its children, all will be implicitly selected.

Oops, I made a mistake, how do I clear everything ?

To clear all the criteria you have selected, click on the "refresh" icon in the left hand side bottom corner.

I'm ready ! How do I start my research ?

When you have selected all the criteria that interests you, press on the big button in the bottom of the screen. Don't be afraid, if you have made a mistake, you will be able to go back, all your selection will be saved !

Results

In this view are displayed the results of the research based on your criteria's selection. Up to 10 vehicles are displayed, ordered by their proximity with your research

Preview

On the right side of the screen is displayed the preview of the currently selected vehicle. It displays some useful informations about the vehicle, such as the overall grade given to the vehicle regarding is proximity to your request. The 5 criteria that matched the best are shown along with their grade.

If you want to learn more about a vehicle, press on the "see more" button in the vehicle's preview

If you want to book a vehicle, press on the button "Book" in the vehicle's preview.

If the results aren't interesting to you, you can go back to the selection of the criterias view by pressing on the left arrow in the menu.

Settings

The settings view allow you to control your informations. You can change your name, your password or delete your account. To go back to the previews view, click on the back icon in the menu.

How do I change my name ?

To change your name, press on the edit icon on the right of your current name. You will then be prompted to type in a new name. Press on the tick icon to validate the change. Press on the cross icon to cancel the change.

How do I change my password ?

To change your password, press on the big green button that reads "Change my password". You will then be prompted to type in your current password for security reasons and your new password two times to prevent typing errors. Press on the tick icon to validate the change or on the cross icon to cancel it.
Do not lose the new password, you will need it to connect to the application !

How do I delete my account ?

To delete your account, press on the big green button that reads "delete my account". You will be asked to type in your password and confirm your choice. Be aware that this action is irreversible. All your booking will be lost.

Booking

This view allows you to book one or more vehicles for a defined time period. Type in the amount of vehicles your want then pick in the date picker the dates between which you want to have the vehicle(s).
To validate the booking, press the "book" button. To cancel and close the view, click on the back icon in the menu or the "cancel" button.

Detailed view

In this view is displayed all the informations about a vehicle. The top part describes the vehicle and states its name.

Specifications

The bottom part gives all the technical specifications of the vehicle.

Universe

The right panel gives a brief description of the universe (fictionnal or not) where the vehicle belongs.

Booking the vehicle

To book the vehicle, press on the "book this vehicle" button. The price of the vehicle is displayed per day.

Booking list

This view allow you to manage your bookings. It lists all your current booked vehicles. You can cancel a booking simply by pressing on the "delete this booking" button. If you want to know more about the vehicle that you booked, press in the "See more" button.
