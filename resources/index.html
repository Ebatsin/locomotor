Authentification

Log in

You simply need to enter your name and your password in the adjacents fields and press the green button "Log in".

Register

You need to click the on the link "Register" at the bottom of the central box. Then enter your name and your password twice and press the green button "Register".

Search

To search for a vehicle that suits you needs, select only the criterias that matter to you.

If a criteria is not important to you, select the big upper box "This criterion is not useful to me".

On the opposite, if a criteria is very important to you, check the small box "This criterion is really important to me" at the bottom-left-hand corner of the window.

There are multiple type of values you can select :

Set

Click on the value that you want, their color will be brighter. To remove a value from your selection, simply click again on it.

Interval

Drag the range bounds to select the range you want.

Tree

Same as set selection, click on a node to select it and display its leaves. To remove a node and its children from your selection, simply click again on the node.

Navigation

You can either use the bottom left-hand or right-hand arrow to navigate to the previous of the next criteria or the breadcrumb at the top of the window. With the breadcrumb, you can jump to another category quickly by clicking on the current category or go the another criteria of the current category by clicking on it.

Start the search !

Press the big button at the bottom to start a research !

Results

Here are represented the results of the research based on your criteria' selection. Up to 10 vehicles are displayed, in order of proximity from your request.

Quick view

To display a quick view on the right. In this panel, you can see the best criterias that matter you with their grade.

See more

To see more of the specifications of the vehicle, simply click on the button "See more" in the quick view on the right.

Booking

To book a vehicle, simply click on the button "See more" in the quick view on the right.

Back to research

If the results aren't interesting for you, you can go back to selection of the criterias with the left arrow on top-left-corder of the window.
